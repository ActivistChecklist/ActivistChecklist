<Section title="هل تعرّض Signal للاختراق؟ هل هو آمن؟ هل هو موثوق؟" slug="secure">
  كل بضعة أشهر، تنتشر منشورات تدّعي أن Signal غير آمن أو أنه تعرّض للاختراق.

  **هل تعرّض Signal للاختراق؟** [**على الأرجح لا**](https://hassignalbeenhacked.info/)**.**

  للاطلاع على شرح أكثر شمولاً حول أمان Signal، بما في ذلك الملكية غير الربحية، والتشفير، والمصدر المفتوح، وشائعات التمويل، ومخاطر الأجهزة، اقرأ مقالتنا: [هل Signal آمن؟](/signal-secure/).

  ثمة مخاطر لاستخدام Signal، لكنها عادةً مرتبطة بطريقة الاستخدام الآمن، لا بالتكنولوجيا نفسها.

  على سبيل المثال، إذا تسلّل شخص ما إلى مجموعة Signal خاصة، يمكنه التقاط لقطات شاشة لجميع الرسائل ونشرها على الإنترنت.

  من الممكن أيضاً أن يصادر الشرطة هاتفك ويخترقه إذا كان رمز مرورك ضعيفاً. لكن هذا ليس قصوراً في Signal. جميع رسائلك متاحة إذا تمكّن أي شخص من الوصول الجسدي لهاتفك وفتح قفله. لهذا نوصي باستخدام [رمز مرور قوي](/essentials/) و[تحديث نظام تشغيل جهازك دائماً](/essentials/).
</Section>

<FAQ>
  <FAQItem question="Is Signal actually secure?" answer="Yes. Signal is the most secure mainstream messenger available. Messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted, the code is open source, and Signal is run by a nonprofit that does not store message contents. The biggest risks are usually how Signal gets used, not the technology itself: a weak phone passcode, a group with an infiltrator, or someone taking screenshots." />
  <FAQItem question="Can police read my Signal messages?" answer="Not the message contents. Signal does not have the keys to decrypt them. What Signal can give law enforcement is the account creation date and the date of last connection. Police can also read your messages if they physically have your unlocked phone, so a strong passcode and screen lock matter." />
  <FAQItem question="Is Signal safer than WhatsApp?" answer="For activist use, yes. WhatsApp uses the same encryption protocol Signal invented, but it is owned by Meta, collects detailed metadata about who talks to whom and when, and shares that with Facebook. In response to law enforcement requests, Meta turns over significantly more data than Signal." />
  <FAQItem question="Do I need to verify safety numbers?" answer="For your closest organizing contacts and anyone you discuss sensitive plans with, yes. Verifying once tells Signal you have confirmed this contact is who they say they are. Without verification, a sophisticated attacker who controls the phone network could in theory swap a key. With verification, you would be alerted if that happened." />
  <FAQItem question="Should I use my real phone number on Signal?" answer="If you can use a username instead of your number, that is the more private option. Signal now lets you set a username and hide your number from people who do not already have it in their contacts. Activists who want extra distance can register Signal with a secondary number from MySudo, Google Voice, or a prepaid SIM." />
  <FAQItem question="What is the registration PIN for?" answer="The PIN lets you recover your Signal profile, contacts, and group memberships if you lose your phone or set up Signal on a new device. It also protects against someone who steals your SIM from being able to register Signal as you on their phone. Pick a long PIN, save it in your password manager." />
</FAQ>
