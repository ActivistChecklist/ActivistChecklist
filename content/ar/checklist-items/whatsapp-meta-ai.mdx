For activists, Meta AI is a data collection feature dressed up as a helpful assistant. Anything you ask it about your organizing, your contacts, your locations, or your plans gets stored, profiled, and potentially exposed to law enforcement.

<Alert type="warning">
  You can't entirely disable Meta AI in WhatsApp. The best protection is to never use it, and to never tag **@MetaAI** in a group chat. Tagging it pulls the surrounding messages into Meta's systems.
</Alert>

To keep Meta AI out of specific group chats, also enable [Advanced Chat Privacy](/whatsapp/#whatsapp-advanced-chat-privacy) on those chats.
