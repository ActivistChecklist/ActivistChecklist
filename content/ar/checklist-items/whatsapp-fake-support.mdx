There has been an increase in fake messages claiming to be from "WhatsApp Support" that try to hijack your account.

**Remember: WhatsApp will never DM you asking for your PIN or 6-digit verification code.** If you get one, it's a phishing attempt.

If you're not sure whether a message is legit, don't reply, don't tap any links, and don't enter any codes. Instead, contact WhatsApp through the in-app **Help** section.
