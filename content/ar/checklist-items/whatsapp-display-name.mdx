---
title: Don't use your real name or photo on your WhatsApp profile
preview: |
  Helps protect you from cops or attackers who want to de-anonymize you. Especially useful if you are in unvetted WhatsApp groups.
firstPublished: 2026-05-22
lastUpdated: 2026-05-29
---

A lot of community organizing happens in unvetted groups on WhatsApp where you don't know how much you can trust everyone in the group. These groups can be infiltrated by [right-wing adversaries](https://nitter.net/camhigby/status/2015093531526729789#m) and [law enforcement](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/nov/21/fbi-signal-group-chat-immigration).

Using a fake name or just your first name prevents someone from finding your real identity through a Google search. Using a photo that isn't your face prevents someone from finding your social profiles based on image search or facial recognition. Both steps help defend you against [doxxing](/doxxing/) and online harassment.

<Alert type="warning">
  It's very hard to be fully anonymous on WhatsApp because your phone number is visible to anyone you message and anyone you're in a group with. If a group has a bad actor, they can easily use your phone number to [deanonymize you](/doxxing/).
</Alert>

<HowTo title="How to change your profile display name">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > Tap **You** in the bottom bar > Tap your name > Tap your name again > edit your name to be a fake name or just your first name > Tap **Save**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Settings** > Tap your name at the top > Tap your name again > edit your name to be a fake name or just your first name > Tap **Save**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

<HowTo title="How to change your profile photo">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > Tap **You** in the bottom bar > Tap your profile picture > Tap **Edit** underneath the profile picture > Upload a generic photo that you found online that doesn't relate to your identity, preferences, interests, or location</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Settings** > Tap your profile picture > Tap **Edit** underneath the profile picture > Upload a generic photo that you found online that doesn't relate to your identity, preferences, interests, or location</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

Remind people in your groups to do this as well. Security is a team sport.
