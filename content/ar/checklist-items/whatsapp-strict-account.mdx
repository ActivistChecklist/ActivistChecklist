preview: |
  Turns on two-step verification, security notifications, unknown-sender blocking, link preview disabling, and silenced unknown callers in one step.

Enabling Strict Account Settings turns on a number of protections at once. It's a good default for anyone doing activism.

<HowTo title="How to enable Strict Account Settings">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > Tap **You** in the bottom bar > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Strict account settings** > **Next** > **Turn on**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Strict account settings** > **Next** > **Turn on**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

<Collapsible title="If you want to enable each setting manually instead of using Strict Account Settings, here are the steps">
  You only need this if you'd rather pick and choose individual settings instead of using the one-tap Strict Account Settings toggle above. If you already enabled Strict Account Settings, you can skip this.

  <InlineChecklist>
    * **Two-step verification.** <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Two-step verification** > set a PIN</MenuPath>
    * **Security notifications.** <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Security notifications** > toggle on</MenuPath>
    * **Block media from unknown senders.** <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Block media from unknown contacts** > toggle on</MenuPath>
    * **Disable link previews.** <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > **Advanced** > **Disable link previews** > toggle on</MenuPath>
    * **Silence unknown callers.** <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > **Calls** > **Silence unknown callers** > toggle on</MenuPath>
    * **Lock profile visibility to contacts only** (last seen, about, profile photo, status). <MenuPath inline>WhatsApp > **Privacy** > set each one to **My Contacts** or **Nobody**</MenuPath>
  </InlineChecklist>
</Collapsible>

<Alert type="warning">
  Remember to save your two-step verification PIN somewhere safe, like your [password manager](/essentials/#password-manager). If you lose it, you can be locked out of your account.
</Alert>
