Advanced Chat Privacy is a per-chat toggle that blocks chat export, disables auto-downloading media, and blocks Meta AI features in that specific chat.

You must enable it for each sensitive chat. It can't be set automatically across all chats.

<HowTo title="How to enable Advanced Chat Privacy">
  <MenuPath>Open the chat > tap the chat name at the top > **Advanced chat privacy** > toggle on</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

Unless a group admin has restricted group settings to admins only, you can enable this in groups where you're a regular member, which helps protect everyone's privacy.

<Alert type="info">
  Turning this on will also disable photos and videos in that chat from being automatically saved to your camera roll.
</Alert>
