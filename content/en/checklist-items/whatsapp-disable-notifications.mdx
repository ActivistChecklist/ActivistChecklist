title: Hide WhatsApp notification content on the lock screen

If your phone is confiscated, your notification history can reveal message content and sender names, even after the messages themselves have disappeared. See the [Prairieland case](https://bsky.app/profile/activistchecklist.org/post/3mityctmsfc2v) for why this matters.

Apple has resolved the root of this issue on recent versions of iOS, but it's unclear whether Android phones still present the same weakness, so it's worth hiding previews on either platform.

<HowTo title="How to hide WhatsApp notification content">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* **Settings** app > **Notifications** > **WhatsApp** > set **Show Previews** to **Never**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** app > **Apps** > **WhatsApp** > **Notifications** > turn off **Show notification content on lock screen**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>
