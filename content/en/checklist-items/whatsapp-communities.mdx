WhatsApp has a newer feature called **Communities** that lets you create a parent group containing multiple subgroups.

The benefit for organizers is that Communities have a "phone number privacy" feature so that other members can't see each other's phone numbers.

<Alert type="info">
  Phone number privacy in Communities has limits: group admins can still see your phone number, and so can anyone who already has it saved in their contact book.
</Alert>

If you're running a large organizing group on WhatsApp, consider migrating to a Community to reduce the risk of members being [doxxed](/doxxing/) by other members or infiltrators.
