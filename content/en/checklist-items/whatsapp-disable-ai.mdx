Apple Intelligence and Android's notification summaries can send parts of your notifications to cloud servers. It's not fully clear what gets sent where, and the data could be compelled by a government request.

We have an article about this topic as it relates to Signal. The same reasoning applies to WhatsApp: [Is Apple Intelligence reading my Signal messages?](/signal-ai/)

<HowTo title="How to disable AI notification summaries for WhatsApp">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* **Settings** app > **Notifications** > **Summarize Notifications** > toggle off **WhatsApp**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** app > **Notifications** > **Notification summaries** > add **WhatsApp** to the excluded list</MenuPath>
</HowTo>
