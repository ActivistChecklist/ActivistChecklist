The best thing you can do to get a sense of how important it is to lock down your public profile is to search for yourself.

Later on in this guide, we will have you do a detailed manual Google search. But for now, **the purpose is just to learn what private information about you is easy to access**.

<HowTo title="How to do an initial search on Google for your private info - try to find your">
  Search Google for the following, replacing the example values with your real info:

  * `"555-867-5309"` (your phone number)
  * `"Jamie Garcia" address` (your name plus the word "address")
  * `"Jamie Garcia" phone number` (your name plus the word "phone number")
  * `"Jamie Garcia" "Springfield High School"` (your name plus your high school or college)
  * `"Jamie Garcia" employer` (your name plus the word "employer," or try the name of your actual workplace)

  Using quotes is helpful but not required. If you can't find anything, try without quotes.

  Your task: **How long does it take you to find your home address?**

  Most likely not very long. The rest of this guide will help you remove a lot of this information.
</HowTo>
