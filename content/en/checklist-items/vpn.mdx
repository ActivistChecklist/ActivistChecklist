Anytime you connect to the internet (phone or computer), your internet provider is revealing your approximate location to every site/app you use.

We know that [law enforcement agencies are using tools to track your location](https://www.404media.co/inside-ices-tool-to-monitor-phones-in-entire-neighborhoods/) using data gathered from apps you have and ads you see. Using a VPN with ad-blocking features enabled makes it much harder for them to track you. Also, police can get a subpoena for your internet traffic from your internet provider.

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) helps mask your location and makes you slightly harder to identify.

A VPN with ad-blocking enabled on your phone can help prevent warrantless location tracking (through ad networks).

Check out our dedicated [VPN page](/vpn/) for more info on when a VPN is actually useful and when it is not.

<Alert type="warning">
  **A VPN doesn't make you fully anonymous.** For sensitive browsing requiring higher anonymity, consider [Tor Browser](https://activistchecklist.org/research) as a second browser alongside your everyday one.
</Alert>

**Options:** These are the only VPNs we recommend activists use:

* [**IVPN**](https://www.ivpn.net/en/) **(top recommendation)**: Easy to use and renews automatically.
* [**Proton VPN**](https://protonvpn.com/) has a solid free plan, but it is only for 1 device. To get ad-blocking, you need a paid plan. (If you use Proton, see our [guide to using Proton safely](/proton/).)

<HowTo title="How to set up IVPN">
  1. **Generate your account:** Go to IVPN and click [Generate IVPN Account](https://www.ivpn.net/en/pricing/) ($6/mo or $60/yr). Under **Standard Plan** click **Select** (choose the **Pro Plan** if you have more than 2 devices).
  2. **Write down your Account ID:** Save it somewhere safe like your password manager. There is no "forgot password" option, so if you lose it, no one can recover it for you.
  3. **Pay:** Select monthly or yearly, check the **Automatic renewal** box, enter your payment details, and click **Make Payment**.
     * Instead of a credit card, you can also order a [voucher card for IVPN](https://digitalgoods.proxysto.re/en) so that your identity is even more protected.
  4. **Install the desktop app** ([Mac](https://www.ivpn.net/en/apps-macos/), [Windows](https://www.ivpn.net/en/apps-windows)) and sign in with your Account ID.
  5. **Enable autoconnect:** <MenuPath inline>Find IVPN in your toolbar > **Show IVPN** > ⚙️ **Gear Icon** > **Settings** > **General**</MenuPath> and enable **Launch at login**, **Autoconnect on launch**, and **Allow background daemon to manage autoconnect**.
  6. **Enable ad-blocking:** Turn on **AntiTracker** in the main IVPN app screen to block ads (which can help you keep your location private, especially on your phone).
  7. **Install the mobile app** ([iPhone](https://www.ivpn.net/en/apps-ios), [Android](https://www.ivpn.net/en/apps-android)) and sign in with the same Account ID, then turn on the same settings listed above. (Autoconnect is not a setting on iPhones because it is on by default.)

  We recommend keeping your VPN on at all times unless you're having trouble connecting to a site (see our list of [downsides to using a VPN](/vpn/#downsides)).
</HowTo>
