WhatsApp's multi-device feature is the single biggest account-takeover vector right now. If a website or stranger walks you through "verifying" by entering a code in WhatsApp, that's not verification. That's you linking an attacker's device to your account, giving them full access to your chats.

The same trick works with QR codes. Only scan a WhatsApp QR code when **you yourself** just opened WhatsApp Web or Desktop.

You can audit your account at any time. See [Check Linked Devices regularly](/whatsapp/#whatsapp-linked-devices).
