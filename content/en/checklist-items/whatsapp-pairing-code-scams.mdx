**What is "Linked Devices"?** WhatsApp lets you use your account on other devices (a computer, a tablet, a second phone) by linking them through a QR code or pairing code. Most people never use this feature. See WhatsApp's [official explainer](https://faq.whatsapp.com/1324084875126592/?cms_platform=web) if you want more background.

Attackers have figured out that they can trick you into linking *their* device to your account. If a website or stranger walks you through "verifying" by entering a code in WhatsApp, that's not verification. That's you giving them full access to your chats. The same trick works with QR codes.

So: **never enter a pairing code or scan a WhatsApp QR code because a website or person asked you to.** Only scan a WhatsApp QR code when **you yourself** just opened WhatsApp Web or Desktop.

You can audit your account at any time. See [Check Linked Devices regularly](/whatsapp/#whatsapp-linked-devices).
