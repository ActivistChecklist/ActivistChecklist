title: Generate a random passphrase for your laptop (if you're bringing it)
titleBadges: []
---
The encryption on your devices is only as strong as your passphrase. If you think of a passphrase yourself, it is likely much easier to crack than one that is randomly generated.

<HowTo title="How to set a randomly-generated laptop password">
  1. Generate a computer-generated, random passphrase using a site like [StrongPhrase.net](https://strongphrase.net/more)
  2. Change your password on your device:

     <MenuPath>*macOS:* **System Settings** > **Users & Groups** > Click the circle "i" icon ℹ️ next to your account > Click **Change...**</MenuPath>

     Then use the passphrase you generated above.

     <MenuPath>*Windows:* **Settings** > **Accounts** > **Sign-in options** > **Password** > **Change** > Enter current password</MenuPath>

     Then use the passphrase you generated above.
</HowTo>
