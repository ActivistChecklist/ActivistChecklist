WhatsApp's multi-device feature is a major pathway for an attacker to takeover your account. Attackers trick you into scanning a QR code or entering a pairing code, and their computer can start seeing all the messages you send and receive.

The same multi-device infrastructure has also been used by mercenary spyware to target journalists and organizers. See our [spyware page](/spyware/) for more.

<HowTo title="How to check your linked devices">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > Tap **You** in the bottom bar > **Linked Devices**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Linked Devices**</MenuPath>

  Log out anything you don't recognize. If you don't use WhatsApp Web or Desktop, log out of everything.
</HowTo>
