We know that [ICE and other law enforcement agencies are using location tracking tools](https://www.404media.co/inside-ices-tool-to-monitor-phones-in-entire-neighborhoods/) that partially rely on the "advertising ID" that your phone provides. These tools are often using these to repress social movements.

iPhones have this feature disabled by default. On Android, it is on by default and needs to be disabled.

<HowTo title="How to disable advertising ID">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* **Settings** > **Privacy & Security** > **Tracking** > **Disable app tracking**</MenuPath>

  *By default, iPhones already have this setting disabled. But you can double-check to make sure.*

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** > Click the search icon and search for "ads" or "advertising" > Tap **Delete advertising ID**</MenuPath>

  *This setting is different on different phones, so searching is easiest.*
</HowTo>
