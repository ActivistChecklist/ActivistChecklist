Cops can try to [force you to unlock your phone with your face/fingerprint](https://theintercept.com/2026/01/30/washington-post-hannah-natanson-fbi-biometrics-unlock-phone/) (though this is supposed to require a warrant, but that doesn't mean that always happens).

You have [stronger legal standing](https://ssd.eff.org/module/attending-protest#remove-fingerprint-or-face-unlock) with a passcode than with biometric unlock.

<HowTo title="How to disable face/fingerprint unlock">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* **Settings** > **Face ID & Passcode** > Disable **Use Face ID for iPhone Unlock**</MenuPath>

  *(You can leave the rest enabled.)*

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** > **Lock Screen** (or **Security**) > **Biometrics and Security** > **Disable both Face Recognition and Fingerprint Unlock** for unlocking your phone</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

Caveat: Face/fingerprint unlock can make it easier to use a strong passcode without typing it every time. If you feel you have to choose between a long passcode and disabling biometrics, we recommend a longer passcode.

<Alert type="success">
  **iPhone locking tip:** To force your passcode on the next unlock, either press the side button 5 times rapidly, or hold the side button and either volume button for a few seconds.

  Test first: depending on your settings, these gestures may also start a countdown to call emergency services. You can [change that](https://support.apple.com/guide/personal-safety/emergency-call-text-iphone-apple-watch-ips4f0cd709b/web).

  Note: Doing this does not protect your data from phone hacking tools that cops use. The only way to do that is to fully power off your device.
</Alert>

<Alert type="success">
  **Android locking tip:** On most Android phones, you can temporarily disable face/fingerprint unlock by holding down the power button and tapping “Lockdown” on the menu that appears.
</Alert>

<Alert type="info">
  **iPhone camera tip:** You can take photos without unlocking your phone by **swiping left** on your home screen. (In case you get arrested while filming.)

  **Android camera tip:** For most Android phones, you can open the camera without unlocking your phone by double-pressing the power button.
</Alert>
