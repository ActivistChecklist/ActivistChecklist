---
Google and Apple haven't made it clear when their AI tools are sending our private information (like Signal messages) off of our device onto their services. If they are sent to their servers, they are at risk of being accessed via a government court order.

Our understanding is that these AI features ***currently*** only summarize notifications. But with the way things are going, they might try to be more invasive.

Read our article on [Is Apple Intelligence reading my Signal messages?](/signal-ai/)

<HowTo title="Option A: How to disable AI notifications summaries just for Signal">
  #### Option A: Disable AI notifications summaries just for Signal

  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* Open the **Settings** app > **Notifications** > **Summarize Notifications** > Toggle off **Signal**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** > **Notifications** > **Notification summaries** > **Manage apps** (under **Don't include these apps**) > Toggle off **Signal**</MenuPath>

</HowTo>

<HowTo title="How to entirely disable AI notification summaries">
  #### Option B: Entirely Disable AI notification summaries

  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* Open the **Settings** app > **Apple Intelligence & Siri** > Toggle off **Apple Intelligence**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* Open **Settings** > **Notifications** > Toggle off **Use notification summaries** (or **Notification summaries**)</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Mac:*  > **System Settings** > **Apple Intelligence & Siri** > Toggle off **Apple Intelligence** > **Confirm**</MenuPath>

  *(Mac requires an Apple computer with an M1 processor or newer.)*
</HowTo>
