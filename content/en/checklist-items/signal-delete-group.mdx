When you close a Signal group, the chat history doesn't disappear from members' devices. It stays there until each person manually deletes it. For an active organizing group, that can mean hundreds of phones holding a full record of your coordination, indefinitely.

This process gives members a chance to clean up the chat themselves, then removes anyone who didn't. It takes about 10 minutes of active work, plus a day of waiting. Before you start, update Signal on all your linked devices. Complete steps 1 through 7 in one sitting before pausing.

<HowTo title="How to safely close a large Signal group (10+ members)">
  **Phase 1: Give members a chance to leave**

  1. **Rename the group** to something that tells members what's happening:
  `1. LEAVE  2. DELETE`

  2. **Lock down permissions:** tap the group name > **Permissions** > set all three to **Only Admins**

  3. **Remove other admins:** revoke admin privileges from anyone else in the group

  4. **Turn off the group link:** tap the group name > **Group Link** > turn off. Revoke outstanding invites and reject pending requests.

  5. **Set disappearing messages to 1 week**

  6. **Clear the group description**

  7. **Send this message** (you can copy and paste from Signal to preserve bold formatting):

  > Hi all, we're shutting down this chat.
  >
  > **Step 1: Leave the group**
  > *iPhone or Android:* tap the group name, scroll down, tap **Leave Group**
  > *Desktop:* click the three dots, click **Leave group**
  >
  > **Step 2: Delete the chat from your device**
  > *iPhone:* swipe the chat left in your chat list, tap **Delete**
  > *Android:* press and hold the chat in your chat list, tap **Delete**
  > *Desktop:* open the chat, click the three dots, click **Delete**

  **Phase 2: Remove remaining members**

  8. **Wait one day**, then continue.

  9. **Rename the group** to: `DELETE`

  10. **Send this message:**

  > Hi all, I'm removing everyone from this chat now. You don't need to do anything except delete the chat from your device once you've been removed.
  >
  > **How to delete:**
  > *iPhone:* swipe the chat left in your chat list, tap **Delete**
  > *Android:* press and hold the chat, tap **Delete**
  > *Desktop:* open the chat, click the three dots, click **Delete**

  11. **Remove all remaining members** one by one.

  12. **Leave the group and delete it** from all of your devices.
</HowTo>

<Alert type="info">
  For smaller groups (under 10 members), you can skip Phase 1 and go straight to removing members and sending the Phase 2 message.
</Alert>
