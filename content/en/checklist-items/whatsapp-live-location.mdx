WhatsApp lets you share your real-time location with a person or group for a set amount of time. It's easy to turn on and forget about, especially if you started sharing with someone hours ago.

For activists, an open live-location share is a direct map of your movements that anyone in the chat can see.

<HowTo title="How to review and stop live location sharing">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Live location** > review the list and tap **Stop sharing** on anything you no longer need</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Live location** > review the list and tap **Stop sharing** on anything you no longer need</MenuPath>
</HowTo>
