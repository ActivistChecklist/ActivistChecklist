title: Hide your "Last seen & online" time on WhatsApp

By default, anyone you've ever exchanged messages with can see when you were last on WhatsApp. For activists, that's a low-effort way for someone to map your patterns of activity.

<HowTo title="How to hide your last seen and online status">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* WhatsApp > Tap **You** in the bottom bar > **Privacy** > **Last seen & online** > Select **Nobody**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* WhatsApp > ⋮ > **Privacy** > **Last seen & online** > Select **Nobody**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>

<Alert type="info">
  The "Strict Account Settings" toggle locks this setting, so change Last seen first if you want a custom value.
</Alert>
