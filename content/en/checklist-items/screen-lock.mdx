title: Enable "Screen Lock" and "Hide in App Switcher"
preview: |
  If you hand your phone to someone, Screen Lock is an extra layer of security.
titleBadges: []
---
* **Screen Lock:** If you hand your phone to someone while unlocked, this ensures they must provide the face/fingerprint/PIN unlock again before opening Signal.
* **Hide in App Switcher:** This will hide your messages when switching between apps.

<HowTo title="How to hide screen in App Switcher">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* Signal > **Settings** > **Privacy** > Enable **Screen Lock** then enable **Hide Screen in App Switcher**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* Signal > **Settings** > **Privacy** > Enable **Screen lock** then enable **Screen Security**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>
