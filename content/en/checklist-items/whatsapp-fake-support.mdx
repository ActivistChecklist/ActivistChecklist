Your WhatsApp PIN and the 6-digit verification code WhatsApp texts you are the keys to your account. Anyone who gets them can take it over.

**Never share these codes with anyone, for any reason.** This applies even if the person says they're from "WhatsApp Support" or "WhatsApp Security". WhatsApp will never DM you, call you, or text you asking for your PIN or verification code. If someone asks, it's a phishing attempt.

There has been an increase in fake messages claiming to be from "WhatsApp Support" that try to hijack your account this way. If you're not sure whether a message is legit, don't reply, don't tap any links, and don't enter any codes. Instead, contact WhatsApp through the in-app **Help** section.
