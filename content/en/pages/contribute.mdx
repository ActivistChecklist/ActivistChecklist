We are a small, all-volunteer open-source project. 

We have a whole site dedicated to helping contributors get started on the project: **[Activist Checklist Contributor Guide](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/)**.

## Getting oriented

New to contributing? Read the overview, code of conduct, and privacy notes in the guide.

Also on the guide: [Code of conduct](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/getting-started/code-of-conduct/) · [Protecting your privacy](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/getting-started/protecting-your-privacy/)

## Make a suggestion

You can make a suggestion for the site by creating an [issue on Github](https://github.com/ActivistChecklist/ActivistChecklist/issues) (requires a GitHub account) or through our [contact form](https://activistchecklist.org/contact/).

## Editing and writing

We need help with editing existing guides and writing new ones.

<Button title="Start editing or writing" href="https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/start-writing/" target="_blank" icon="IoOpenOutline" iconPosition="right" size="lg" />

We maintain a living list of [guide ideas and priorities](https://github.com/ActivistChecklist/ActivistChecklist/wiki/Guide-proposals) on the wiki to see what we’re hoping to publish next. If one of these interst you, please [help us write it](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/writing-a-guide-from-scratch/).

Also: [Using the visual editor](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/visual-editor/) · [Style guide](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/style/) · [Page structure and formatting](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/structure/) · [Writing a guide from scratch](https://docs.activistchecklist.org/writing/writing-a-guide-from-scratch/)

## Translating

All of our translations are automatic. What we need your help with is editing and approving the automatic translations. We coordinate human review of translations on [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/activistchecklist). The guide explains the workflow and what “reviewed” means on the live site.

## Other ways you can contribute

Beyond writing, translating, and code, we occasionally need help with **social media**, **copy editing**, **UX feedback**, **graphic design**, **security review** of guides as tools change, and **getting the word out** (toolkits, networks, workshops, and [hosting a security party](/party/)). If one of these fits you, [contact us](/contact/).

Hosting and translation work have real costs. [Get in touch](/contact/) if you want to help fund the project. We do not have a public donation system yet; contributions are not tax-deductible.
