---
title: Enable "Always Relay Calls"
titleBadges: []
---

By default, Signal calls connect directly between you and the other person. This means the other person's device can see your IP address. With a court order to your internet provider, your IP address can be used to identify you specifically.

Enabling "Always Relay Calls" routes your calls through Signal's servers, hiding your IP address from the other party. (If you already use a [VPN](/essentials/#vpn), your IP address is already masked.)

<HowTo title="How to enable Always Relay Calls">
  * *On iPhone:* Signal > Profile picture > Privacy > Advanced > **Toggle on "Always Relay Calls"**
  * *On Android:* Signal > Profile picture > Privacy > Advanced > **Toggle on "Always Relay Calls"**
</HowTo>
