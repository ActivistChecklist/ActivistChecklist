<HowTo title="Cómo desactivar los asistentes de voz">
  *En iPhone:* Settings > Siri > **Desactiva "Allow Siri When Locked"**

  *En Android:* Settings > Google > Settings for Google apps > Search, Assistant & Voice > Google Assistant > **Desactiva "Lock screen"**
</HowTo>
