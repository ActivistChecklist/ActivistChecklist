<HowTo title="Cómo desactivar los asistentes de voz">
  <MenuPath>*iPhone:* **Settings** > **Siri** > Disable **Allow Siri When Locked**</MenuPath>

  <MenuPath>*Android:* **Settings** > **Google** > **Settings for Google apps** > **Search, Assistant & Voice** > **Google Assistant** > Disable **Lock screen**</MenuPath>
</HowTo>
